Our Story

Why we do what we do

We decided to create Kare4Kidz because when we were younger, our parents would leave us at daycare because they had work. We decided to pursue this idea to take care of children like we once were and make sure they are in a safe environment. All of our workers are well qualified and educated from college in their field of work.

Our company is located in the Bronx, where we aim to make the lives of parents a little easier.
