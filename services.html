Our Services

Our services include daycare services, babysitting services, and an early learning program.

Daycare Services

In our daycare we allow parents of children 9 months to 3 years to bring their child from 6:00AM and to pick them up by 6:00PM.

Early Learning Program

At our daycare we offer early learning lessons for kids age 1 to 3 years old.

Homemade & Fresh Baby Food

We also offer homemade baby food made by our talented chef TJ for parents who can't get their own food.
We also have jars available for parents to purchase.

Children's Library

We have a child's library full of educational and fantasy books for your children to enjoy and get a jumpstart on their reading.

Babysitting Service



Last but not least, we offer babysitting services incase you are unable to bring your child to daycare.
